There is a lot of misunderstanding and confusion among people regarding different speed units  like kbps , Kbps .kb/s etc etc  so in this post will try to cover all of this in a simple and understandable manner
To begin with  :



First of all, note the capitalization of letters in the words.

Kbps - Kilo Bits per second
Mbps - Mega Bits per second

[![](https://qph.ec.quoracdn.net/main-qimg-f49267de16345788dd651422c425dde1-p)](https://qph.ec.quoracdn.net/main-qimg-f49267de16345788dd651422c425dde1-p)

While KB/s or MB/s is what you see in browsers or download managers.

**This capital B stands for Bytes.**

Now, 8 bits = 1 byte. That means your 4 Mbps connection (4 Mega Bits per second) gives you 4/8 = 512 KB/s (512 Kilo BYTES per second
