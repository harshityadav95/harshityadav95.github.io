Since the Lockdown got implemented in India and school and colleges got shutdown , it took months for everyone to plan and find the right tools to continue the Lectures and progress with the ongoing semester  , my college also took a months time and decideded to use Microsoft teams as a platform for conducting classes and finishing of the planned lectures and using  it to conduct assignements by taking in submission in form of scanned handwritten answerscripts , which was well accepted and easy to use .

As the time went on and University remained closed Nationwide my College looked on for solutions to conduct the exams Online using a software called Pexa Lite by   company called [Little More Innovation Labs](http://www.littlemoreinnovation.com/pexa-lite/) and released the datesheets and notice for moving forward with the semester and conducting midterms which was ok but let to  huge clash between students and college administration  on the following points :

- The Software was first time being field tested and had truck load of bugs from crashing and freezing , being shown as virus in all antivirus software including windows and antivirus software had to be disabled to use it
- Only worked on Windows OS and consumed lot of bandwidth which was not an option for students whose connectivity was being affected by natural calamities
- The Input method for answers was inadequate with no support for tables , charts , diagrams , formulas
- The software & college expected us to write those answeres which involved the above and scan the text using webcam software inside the software which was like a cherry on top of the already bad webcam quality windows laptop have and then even detorating it even further with poor software to capture it  , there is a reason ppl use mobile to scan pages not webcam .
