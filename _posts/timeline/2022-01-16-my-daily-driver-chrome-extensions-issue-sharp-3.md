## My Daily Driver Chrome Extensions — Issue #3

![Medium article image](/assets/img/posts/my-daily-driver-chrome-extensions-issue-sharp-3/medium-image-cae8f1625a2b.jpg)

My Daily Driver Chrome Extensions :

**1. BitWarden**

Sign in with Google or Apple is relief when juggling between multiple devices and machines and even browsers, and not every app I want to sign with my primary google account or every new Product hunt launched an app so still have to set login username and password, which I don’t want to remember or use the same password across all services, so I use Bitwarden which is a free open source password management service, works across all platform and devices and no upgrade to premium for normal users

Link:[https://bitwarden.com/](https://bitwarden.com/)

**2. Picture-in-Picture**

Even with an External monitor setup there are times when you want the coding tutorial or space launch event stream on top of your main screen as an overlay and save the real estate on screen for some other tab, this extension by google saves the day

Link:[Chrome Extension Link](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/picture-in-picture-extens/hkgfoiooedgoejojocmhlaklaeopbecg?hl=en)

**3 . Vide Speed Controller**

What started as a productivity hack trick experiment after coming across it in one of[Siraj Raval Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hKBZjH7Ot2g)during college turned out to be best trick of the book that helped me get used to watch and go through courses and podcasts and videos at much faster pace, what this extension provides is a hotkey [S][D] and many more to easily increase and decrease the speed of videos on increments of 0.1x , and works across all sites and videos

Link :[Chrome Extension Link](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/video-speed-controller/nffaoalbilbmmfgbnbgppjihopabppdk?hl=en)

**4 . uBlock Origin**

I still don’t know how people watch Youtube on mobile with a way to many required ads , but on Desktop more than the time it saves the precious memory in our monkey head it saves by not watching that catchy food delivery app advt commercial, also blocks ads on sites as well

Link :[Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/ublock-origin/cjpalhdlnbpafiamejdnhcphjbkeiagm?hl=en)

Drop me a message if you know any chrome extension that you use daily :)
