## What Gets a Faster Reply

- Specific technical feedback with a link to the post or project.

- Practical questions about infrastructure, backend systems, cloud, ML experiments, or engineering learning paths.

## What Usually Gets Ignored

- Guest-post outreach that has nothing to do with this blog.
- Messages with no context beyond "hi".
